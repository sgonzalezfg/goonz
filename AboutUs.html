About Us
GOONZ was created by
Sofia Gonzalez,
an Architecture student,
who wanted to bring art and nature
to everyones homes.


Looking to create a
more affordable option
to take furniture to the next level,
GOONZ started as a personal
solution for her own furnitures,
which then grew into a
Furniture Studio,
with native materials and
art inspiration
